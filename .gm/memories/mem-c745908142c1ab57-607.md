gm-method (witnessed): the COMPLETE four-observation gate counts mutables with status 'known' or 'resolved' as NOT-witnessed -- only 'witnessed' satisfies it. A mutable-add with status:'resolved' inline does NOT clear the gate; you must dispatch mutable-resolve {mutable_id, witness_evidence} which sets 'witnessed'. Audit-workflow subagents driving their own gm chains on the SHARED spool leave their mutables behind in the parent store, so after a fan-out the parent's COMPLETE gate can deny on subagent-spawned mutables -- resolve each by its real id (an auto 'mut-<ts>' id when mutable_id lookup fails).
