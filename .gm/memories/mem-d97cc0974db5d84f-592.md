gm-method (witnessed): in a system where an LLM agent turn can rewrite a row's mutable fields (tags, and even the whole report) AFTER a deterministic pre-turn flag is set, a once-only signal must be recorded as an append-only event/observation, NOT only as a tag -- the agent's own update clobbers the tag. The end-to-end test must assert on the durable observation, not the tag. Witnessed when a test's stub model overwrote both the report (masking a conflict the deterministic detector had just found) and the tags; the append-only observation survived and was the correct assertion target.
