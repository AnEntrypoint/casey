So casey POSTs to /channels/<authorId>/messages which is not a channel -> Discord errors -> .then(r=>r.json()) at handler.js:104 does NOT throw on non-2xx -> adapter.send resolves -> outbound logged with no send error -> nothing posts. Exactly the observed symptom (CASE-1073 outbound logged, no send-failure observation, nothing in Discord).
