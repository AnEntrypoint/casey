gm-method (witnessed): when spool dispatches silently vanish from in/ with no out/ response despite a fresh .status.json, the cause can be MULTIPLE accumulated watcher processes racing on the spool, and/or a watcher booted from a DRIFTED cwd (its .watcher.log shows 'watching <cwd>/.gm/exec-spool/out/.gm/exec-spool/in' -- a doubled path) so it services the wrong dir while consuming inputs from the right one. Recovery: kill ALL plugkit/spool node processes (not just 'stale' ones -- --kill-stale-watchers spares 'fresh' racers), then boot exactly one watcher from the repo root, and confirm .watcher.log shows the correct single 'watching <repo>/.gm/exec-spool/in' path before re-dispatching.
