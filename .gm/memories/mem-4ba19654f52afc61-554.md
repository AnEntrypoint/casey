gm-method (witnessed): when a repo's dependency policy is explicitly "always resolve latest at install time, never pin" (recorded in AGENTS.md/docs), a bugfix that removes a runtime version-uncertainty shim (e.g. a feature-detect probe made obsolete once the floor version is confirmed always-satisfied) should NOT also add a package.json semver floor as a defensive measure -- that would be an unrequested reversal of the standing never-pin policy.
